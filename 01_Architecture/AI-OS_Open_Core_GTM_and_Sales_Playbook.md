Version: 1.0.0
Status: Draft
Layer: Architecture

Document ID: DOC-ARCH-013
Owner: AI-OS Architecture

---

## Purpose

This playbook defines a repeatable go-to-market and sales motion for AI-OS Open-Core commercialization.

It standardizes qualification, discovery, offer mapping, and conversion flow from initial contact to paid deployment.

---

## Scope

Included:

- GTM positioning and buyer problem framing;
- qualification and discovery workflow;
- opportunity progression from diagnostic to paid packs.

Excluded:

- CRM tooling specifics;
- legal/procurement execution;
- account management beyond initial sale.

---

## 1. Core Positioning

AI-OS is positioned as a practical architecture layer that turns ad-hoc AI experiments into governed, repeatable, production-ready operations.

Primary promise:

- faster time-to-value;
- lower governance risk;
- reusable operating artifacts.

---

## 2. Target Segments

1. teams with active AI pilots but no governance baseline;
2. teams with slow and risky prompt release cycles;
3. departments preparing first production AI workflow.

---

## 3. Sales Flow

| Stage | Goal | Exit Condition |
| ----- | ---- | -------------- |
| Qualification | Confirm fit and urgency | Qualified pain + stakeholder owner identified |
| Discovery | Map current workflows and blockers | Clear problem statement and baseline documented |
| Diagnostic Offer | Propose entry assessment | Diagnostic scope approved |
| Recommendation Readout | Present evidence-based path | Buyer accepts next-step deployment option |
| Paid Pack Conversion | Close first paid module | First pack activation started |

---

## 4. Discovery Questions

- What AI workflows are business-critical today?
- Where do quality and governance failures appear most often?
- What blocks release confidence right now?
- Who owns approval for production AI changes?

Discovery output SHALL produce a conversion recommendation (Governance Pack, PromptOps Pack, or Combined Pack).

---

## 5. Offer Mapping Logic

| Observed Need | Recommended Offer |
| ------------- | ----------------- |
| Weak controls and review discipline | Governance Pack |
| High prompt churn and release instability | PromptOps Pack |
| Both control and release failures | Combined Pack |

---

## 6. Sales Assets (Minimum Set)

1. one-page positioning summary;
2. diagnostic service scope template;
3. pack comparison sheet;
4. implementation timeline visual.

---

## 7. GTM KPI Set

| KPI | Target |
| --- | ------ |
| Discovery-to-diagnostic conversion | >= 50% |
| Diagnostic-to-paid conversion | >= 30% |
| Sales cycle (qualified lead to first paid) | <= 45 days |
| Win rate on qualified opportunities | >= 25% |
